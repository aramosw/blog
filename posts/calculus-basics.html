Intro to Calculus: Limits and Derivatives

2025-11-04

The limit of a function describes its behavior as $x$ approaches a value:

$$ \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = 1. $$

The derivative of $f$ at $x$ is defined by

$$ f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h}. $$
